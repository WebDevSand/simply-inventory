Simple Inventory

Simple Inventory is a simple inventory management software, built to imporove the efficiency of business operations. As the name suggests, the purpose of this software is to keep track of:

  • Current Inventory
  • Inventory Transactions
  • Inventory Valuations
  • User level access control to different inventory functions.
